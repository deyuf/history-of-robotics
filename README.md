# History of Robot

A bilingual editorial chronicle of robotics — from Greek myth and Edo karakuri to Unimate, ASIMO, Atlas, Figure, Optimus and embodied general intelligence.

Two volumes:

- **The main chronicle** (`index.html`) — 81 milestones across eight eras, 1500 BCE → 2026.
- **The humanoid edition** (`humanoid.html`) — fifty years of two legs and two hands, with a current-models roster and a six-machine comparison.

Every event is anchored to a primary source. Content is bilingual (English default, 中文 toggle) and released under [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).
